Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

VoxelChat ("we", "us", or "our") operates the VoxelChat mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use VoxelChat:

Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, and Google account ID. This is used solely for authentication purposes.

Minecraft Account Information: We store the Minecraft usernames and account types (premium or cracked) that you add to the app.

Server Information: We store the Minecraft server addresses and names that you save in the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

• Provide and maintain the VoxelChat service

• Authenticate your identity and manage your account

• Connect your Minecraft bot to servers on your behalf

• Improve our app and services

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard security measures including JWT authentication, HTTPS encryption, and secure database storage.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google Sign-In: For authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Microsoft Authentication: For premium Minecraft account verification. Subject to Microsoft's Privacy Policy.

MongoDB Atlas: For secure data storage.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at support@voxelchat.app.

Children's Privacy

VoxelChat does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@voxelchat.app.
